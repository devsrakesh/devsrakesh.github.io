About

Architecting platforms, end‑to‑end.

Solution-oriented Full-Stack Engineer with over four years of experience designing and delivering production-grade web platforms for clients in India and the United Arab Emirates.

I specialize in AWS cloud architecture using ECS, EKS (Kubernetes), Terraform, and eksctl — with deployments fronted by Application Load Balancer and Route 53, and shipped through GitHub Actions CI/CD pipelines. Across the stack, I work with NestJS, Next.js, Node.js, TypeScript, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL, supported by observability tooling (Prometheus, Grafana, CloudWatch) and real-time integrations (Firebase Cloud Messaging, WhatsApp Business API). I am comfortable owning the full delivery lifecycle, from requirements gathering and system design through deployment, monitoring, and ongoing operations.

Beyond hands-on engineering, I lead and mentor both internal development teams and a dedicated network of freelance developers across partner companies. My day-to-day also spans architecture decisions, code review, client billing and payment workflows, and delivering technical training sessions to client teams when required.
